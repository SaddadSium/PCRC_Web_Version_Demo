Major Events & Collaborations

Thalassemia Contest & Exhibition

On the occasion of World Thalassemia Day 2022, Youth Club of Bangladesh-Dhaka Division organized a photography competition and exhibition to raise awareness about Thalassemia, express sympathy towards the affected and motivate common people to donate blood. A total of 26 photographs of 11 members of the Photography Club of Rouf College were selected for exhibition in the competition

3rd National English Olympiad 2022

English Olympiad is organizing the country's 3rd National English Olympiad with the motto of "Inspiring Leadership" to make our youth competent to be global citizens and take lead in international activities. PCRC is one of the Club Partners in this event. We'd like to thank the authority for choosing us.
