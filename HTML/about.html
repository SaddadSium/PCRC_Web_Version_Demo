Our History

Photography Club of Rouf College-PCRC was an online platform first. We started our very first online journey at the end of 2016. In 2017, we arranged our first online contest of photography. As we didn't have any offline platform, the Founder panel of PCRC proposed to Former Principal Lt. Col (Rtd) Akhter Iqbal sir to give us an offline platform and stand it as a club.

Three Individual Departments:

  • Department of Photography
  • Department of Art
  • Department of Cinematography

We arrange workshops based on various knowledge of photography and art. Our members can earn knowledge about any sector they want. Moreover, we hold many intra/inter college contests and competitions of photography, art and designing both offline and online. We achieved many prizes at different national exhibitions. We've achieved 3 international prizes also.

We made a short film 'Opekkha', based on our liberation war which was directed by Md Ikramuzzaman and acted by our members at 2019. A national art exhibition, named 'RongMoshal' was also being held by PCRC at 21st February, 2020.

After lot of struggle and hard work, now in 2026, we are the biggest online and offline platform among all the clubs of Birshreshtha Munshi Abdur Rouf Public College. We can proudly say that we are PCRC and we love to do our job very professionally.
